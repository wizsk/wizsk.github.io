Privacy Policy — Arabic Lexicons

Short version: we don’t collect your data because we literally can’t.

Alright, here’s the deal.

What we collect

Nothing.

Not your name, not your email, not your “anonymous usage data”, not even how often you open the app at 3am.

We would love to say “we collect minimal data”, but that would be a lie. We collect zero.

Permissions

The app asks for no permissions.

If you see a permission prompt, something is wrong and it is not us.

Internet access

This app does not use the internet. At all.

You can turn on airplane mode, throw your router out the window, and it will still work perfectly.

Ads

No ads.

No banners, no popups, no “rewarded videos”, no sneaky tracking disguised as ads. Just silence.

Your data

Everything stays on your device because there is nothing to send anywhere.

We do not have servers. We do not have a database. We do not even have a place to put your data if we wanted to.

Open source

The project is fully open source under the GPL-3.0 License.

If you do not trust this document, read the code. It is all there, hiding nothing, because there is nothing to hide.

Third parties

No third party services.

No analytics.

No trackers.

No mysterious SDKs doing “helpful” things in the background.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, something has gone very wrong. But realistically, it will just keep saying the same thing.

Final note

If you came here worried about privacy, relax.

This app is about Arabic lexicons, not about you.

If we wanted your data, we would have needed internet first. That should tell you everything.

No data. No tracking. No nonsense.
