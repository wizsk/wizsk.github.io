Features

Truly Offline

No sneaky API calls. No "checking for updates" in the background. It doesn't even ask for internet permissions.

Reader Mode

Just tap a word to get its meaning instantly. Copy-pasting text into a search bar is so 2010s.

Multi-word Search

Search entire phrases or roots. Smart suggestions kick in to help you find exactly what you're looking for.

Featherweight

All 11 dictionaries are compressed into a beautifully optimized ~50MB database. Your storage space is safe with us.

Included Lexicons

English-Arabic Dictionaries

Aratools Arabic–English مباشر

GPL-licensed components of the Aratools Arabic-English dictionary. Based on Tim Buckwalter’s Aramorph Arabic morphological analyzer.

Hans Wehr Dictionary معجم هانز فير

Modern Arabic–English dictionary compiled by Hans Wehr. Widely used academic reference organized by triliteral roots.

Lane’s Lexicon قاموس لين

Comprehensive 19th-century Arabic-English lexicon by Edward William Lane, based on major classical sources.

Arabic Dictionaries

Al-Muʿjam al-Ghani المعجم الغني

Contemporary Arabic dictionary focusing on modern vocabulary and usage.

Al-Sihah (al-Jawhari) الصحاح في اللغة

Classical Arabic dictionary by al-Jawhari (4th century AH), one of the foundational root-based lexicons.

Lisan al-Arab لسان العرب

Major classical Arabic lexicon compiled by Ibn Manzur (7th century AH), drawing from earlier authoritative sources.

Al-Muʿjam al-Muʿasirah المعجم المعاصر

Modern Arabic dictionary emphasizing contemporary terminology and usage.

Al-Muʿjam al-Wasit المعجم الوسيط

Standard modern Arabic dictionary published by the Arabic Language Academy in Cairo.

Al-Qamus al-Muhit القاموس المحيط

Influential classical dictionary by al-Firuzabadi (8th century AH), widely cited in later lexicons.

Maqayis al-Lugha مقاييس اللغة

Root-based semantic analysis by Ibn Faris (4th century AH), reducing each root to its core conceptual meanings.

Mufradat Alfaz al-Qur’an مفردات ألفاظ القرآن

Qur’anic lexicon by al-Raghib al-Isfahani, analyzing vocabulary and semantic nuances of Qur’anic terms.
